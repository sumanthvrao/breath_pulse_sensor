Problem Statement:

 To build a sensor based tool to identify lenght of inhalation and exhalation and your heart rate. To upload the data to a noSQL DataBase like mongoDB. To analyse the data to determine distribution of breathing pattern at different points during the day , and its correlation with heart rate.
